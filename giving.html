Why We Give

The way we use what He has given us reflects the condition of our hearts and serves as a testimony that helps share the gospel with the world. We want to glorify God with every area of our lives, and that includes what we do with our finances. Your giving supports our mission of evangelism, discipleship, church planting, and serving our community here in Oshawa and beyond.
