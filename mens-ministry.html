Men's Ministry

Who We Are

Iron Sharpens
Iron

Men's Ministry at Lighthouse Church is a community where men are challenged to grow in faith, integrity, and godly character. We believe that when men are rooted in God's Word and connected to authentic brotherhood, families are strengthened, communities change, and the Kingdom advances.

Programs, schedule, and leadership contact information coming soon. Stay connected with us on Instagram for the latest announcements and events.
