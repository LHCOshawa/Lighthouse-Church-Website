Children's Ministry

Who We Are

Faith Starts
Here

Children's Ministry at Lighthouse Church is a safe, fun, and Spirit-filled environment where kids discover who God is, experience His love, and grow in faith. We believe every child is a gift from God and deserves to encounter Jesus in a way that's real and age-appropriate.

Programs, schedule, and leadership contact information coming soon. Connect with us for more details.

Follow @lhcoshawa
What We Offer

Growing Together

✦

Faith Foundation

Age-appropriate Bible teaching that lays a solid foundation of faith, helping children understand who God is and how much He loves them.

✦

Fun & Discovery

Engaging worship, games, and creative activities that make church a place kids genuinely want to be every week.

✦

Family Partnership

We believe parents are the primary spiritual influencers in a child's life. We partner with families to reinforce faith at home.
