Our Story

Mission & Vision

"Go therefore and make disciples of all nations."

Matthew 28:19

Who We Are

Lighthouse Church — also known as Lighthouse Christian Fellowship — is a vibrant, Spirit-filled community in Oshawa, Ontario. We exist to bring the light of Christ into our city and beyond, building a church that reflects the love, power, and mission of Jesus. Whether you are exploring faith for the first time or looking for a community to grow in, you are welcome here.

Evangelism

The gospel is meant to be shared beyond the four walls of the church. As Jesus instructed in Mark 16:15, "Go into all the world and preach the gospel to every creature." We are passionate about bringing the hope of Christ to our community and beyond through personal outreach, city-wide evangelism, and global missions.

Mark 16:15

02 / 05

Discipleship

Following Jesus is more than a moment—it's a lifelong journey. We are committed to equipping and training believers to grow in their faith, live in obedience to God, and guide others in their walk with Christ. As Jesus said in Matthew 28:20, "Teach them to observe all things that I have commanded you."

Matthew 28:20
03
03 / 05

Church Planting

A thriving church is a multiplying church. The heart of our mission is to raise up leaders and send them out to establish new churches that reflect God's Kingdom. Matthew 9:37-38 reminds us, "The harvest is plentiful, but the laborers are few." Our vision is to see churches planted that will ignite revival, bring transformation, and reach the nations.

Matthew 9:37-38
04
04 / 05

Conversion

The gospel isn't just information—it's transformation. We preach for a decision, believing in the power of the Holy Spirit to change lives. 1 Peter 1:23 declares, "You have been born again… through the living and abiding word of God." Our church is filled with people whose lives have been radically changed by Jesus, and we continue to invite others into that same life-changing encounter.

1 Peter 1:23
05
05 / 05

The Local Church

God has placed everything necessary to accomplish His will within the local church. Ephesians 1:22-23 calls the church "His body, the fullness of Him who fills everything in every way." We believe in the power of gathering, serving, and building together, knowing that God moves when His people are united in faith and purpose.

Ephesians 1:22-23
